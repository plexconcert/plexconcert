Our Story

We believe every team already has what it needs

We started PlexConcert because we noticed something curious. Remote teams would gather for offsites and come home with photos — but not much else. The surface had been touched. The depths had not.

So we asked: what if a retreat could work the way real change works? Not forced from the outside, but invited from within. Through the gentle precision of hypnosis. Through experiences that speak to the whole person — not just the job title.

Today, every program we create is as unique as the team it serves. From Tokyo to Barcelona, Cape Town to Lisbon — we meet your people wherever they are, and we guide them, naturally, toward where they're ready to go.
